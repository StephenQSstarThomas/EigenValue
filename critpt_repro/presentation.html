Three reproductions

The public set ships every reference answer blanked out, so each result below was derived independently from the physics and cross-checked against known limits.

CH-17High-energy · Condensed matter

SYK zero-temperature entropy

Large-N Majorana Sachdev–Ye–Kitaev model, 4-body random couplings. Compute the residual entropy density S/N to four decimals.

MethodMaldacena–Stanford closed form S₀/N = ½ln2 − ∫₀^{1/q} π(½−x)tan(πx)dx, cross-checked by disorder-averaged finite-N exact diagonalization (Jordan–Wigner, N up to 26).
Result — S/N
0.2324
✓ validated q=2 → 0 exactly · large-q → ½ln2 · ED: T→∞ hits ½ln2, low-T rises with N
CH-31Fluid dynamics

Rayleigh–Bénard onset, mixed boundaries

Bottom wall no-slip + fixed heat flux; top wall free-slip + fixed temperature. Find the critical Rayleigh number and horizontal wavenumber at the onset of convection.

MethodMarginal-stability generalized eigenvalue problem via Chebyshev collocation, minimized over wavenumber. Solver first validated against three textbook cases to 4 significant figures.
Result — Rac , kc
816.7 · 2.21
✓ validated rigid-rigid 1707.76 · free-free 657.5 · rigid-free 1100.65
CH-61Quantum computing

Quantum-walk search, simplex of complete graphs

Continuous-time quantum walk searching a 10,100-vertex graph (101 cliques of 100). Find the evolution time T and success probability P at the marked vertex.

MethodExact 7-dimensional symmetry (equitable-partition) reduction — spectrum matches Meyer–Wong's matrix — then the two-stage protocol (γ=2/M then 1/M), gaps 4/M3/2 and 2/√M reproduced.
Result — T , P
801 · 0.98
✓ validated two H-constructions agree · stage-2 gap = 0.200 exactly
